About Me

I am a full stack web developer with experience in React, Redux, Javascript, jQuery, Rails, and PostgreSQL.
I graduated from the University of Arizona in 2012 with a BFA in Acting and Musical Theater. After several years with a survival job in the restaurant industry, I realized that was all it was. Surviving.
After realizing I craved that intelectual and creative stimulation I decided to make a change. With the encouragement of my friends and family I dived head first into the world of web development, and I haven't looked back since.

Please check out some of my projects below!
